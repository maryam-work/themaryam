Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

At The Maryam, we strive to deliver your personalized gifts with care and speed. We partner with reliable courier services to ensure your order reaches you safely.

1. Delivery Areas

We deliver across India.
Same Day Delivery: Available exclusively in Vadodara and Ahmedabad for orders placed before 12:00 PM.
Standard Delivery: Available for all other cities and pin codes.

2. Shipping Charges

Order Type Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 4-7 Business Days Free
Express Shipping 2-4 Business Days ₹150
Same Day (Vadodara/Ahmedabad) Today (by 9 PM) ₹200

3. Processing Time

Since our products are personalized (customized with names/photos), we require 1-2 days to craft your order before it is shipped. "Ready to Ship" items are dispatched within 24 hours.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can track your package in real-time.
